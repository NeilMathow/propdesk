Frequently Asked Questions

Is Starter Really Free Forever? +
Yes — no credit card, no trial, no expiry. You get one account with full rule tracking and payout tracking free forever.
Can I Switch Plans Anytime? +
Yes — upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.
Does It Work With All Prop Firms? +
We currently support Topstep, Apex, My Funded Futures, Lucid Trading, Take Profit Trader, Tradeify, and FundedNext. More added regularly.
What Happens After The 3-Day Trial? +
You'll be prompted to choose a plan. If you don't, your account drops to Starter — no charges ever without your consent.
Is My Trading Data Secure? +
Yes. We use read-only API connections. We never have access to your credentials or the ability to place trades.
