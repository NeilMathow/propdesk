Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and your rights as a user.

Effective: January 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: May 30, 2025

1. Overview

PropDesk ("PropDesk," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website and platform accessible at propdesk.io (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our platform.

By creating an account or using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the Service.

Plain-language summary: PropDesk is a tracking tool for funded traders. We only collect what we need to run the platform. We do not sell your data, we do not share your trading data without your consent, and we use read-only connections — we cannot and will never place trades on your behalf.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information generated automatically through your use of the Service.

2.1 Account Information. When you create an account, we collect your first and last name, email address, and password. If you sign up via Google OAuth, we receive your name and email address from Google — we never receive or store your Google password. We also collect any profile preferences and account settings you configure within the platform.

2.2 Trading & Financial Data. We collect prop firm account names, account sizes, balances, drawdown levels, and rule parameters that you manually enter or connect via integration. This includes trade logs, payout records, journal entries, and performance notes you add within the platform, as well as evaluation phase, funded account status, and challenge progress data. Where integrations exist, we use read-only API connections — we cannot and will never place, modify, or cancel trades on your behalf.

2.3 Payment Information. For paid subscriptions, billing is handled entirely by Stripe. We receive only a tokenized reference to your payment method and never store your full card number, CVV, or bank account details. We do retain billing records including amounts, dates, and plan type for accounting and legal compliance purposes.

2.4 Usage & Technical Data. We automatically collect information about how you interact with the Service, including pages visited, features used, buttons clicked, and actions taken within PropDesk. We also collect device type, browser type and version, operating system, screen resolution, and IP address, as well as session timestamps, session duration, referral source, navigation paths, and error logs used to diagnose and fix platform issues.

2.5 Communications. We retain messages you send us via the contact form, email, or support channels, as well as any survey or feedback responses you voluntarily submit.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or other jurisdictions with similar requirements, we process your personal data under the following legal bases. We process data under Contractual Necessity where processing is required to perform our contract with you, including creating your account, providing the platform, and processing payments. We process data under Legitimate Interests for our business purposes such as improving the platform, preventing fraud, ensuring security, and communicating service updates, where those interests are not overridden by your rights. We process data under Legal Obligation where required to comply with applicable law, including tax and accounting obligations, court orders, and regulatory requirements. Finally, where required by law, we will obtain your explicit Consent before processing your data for a specific purpose such as sending marketing communications, and you may withdraw that consent at any time without affecting prior processing.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to create, maintain, and manage your PropDesk account, and to display your prop firm rules, drawdown levels, payout history, and performance data accurately. We use it to process subscription payments, manage your billing relationship, and send transactional communications including account confirmation, password resets, billing receipts, and important platform notices. We also use it to respond to your support requests and contact form submissions, to analyze usage patterns and fix bugs, and to develop new features that improve the platform. We may use your information to detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions, abuse, and unauthorized access, and to send you optional product updates and tips — you may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time. We use your information to comply with legal obligations including tax reporting and responding to lawful government requests, and to sell, license, or share aggregated or de-identified data with third-party commercial partners as described in Section 5.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We may share your personal data in the following circumstances. By using the Service, you consent to the data sharing practices described in this section.

5.1 Service Providers

We use Supabase as our database and authentication provider — your account data is stored on Supabase infrastructure hosted on AWS under a Data Processing Agreement. Payments are handled by Stripe, which processes all payment card data under PCI-DSS Level 1 compliance; we never receive or store your full card details. Our website and application are hosted on Vercel, which may process technical request data such as IP addresses and headers as part of delivering the Service. If you choose to sign in with Google OAuth, Google processes your authentication and we receive only your name and email address from Google.

5.2 Legal Disclosures

We may disclose your personal data when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to: (a) comply with applicable law or legal process; (b) respond to a lawful request from a government authority; (c) protect the rights, property, or safety of PropDesk, our users, or the public; or (d) enforce our Terms of Service.

5.3 Sale or Licensing of Data

We may sell, license, or otherwise transfer aggregated, anonymized, or pseudonymized usage data and trading behavior data to third parties, including analytics providers, financial data companies, and other commercial partners. Such data will be de-identified to the extent reasonably practicable and will not directly identify you by name or email address. We may also share personal data with third-party commercial partners where you have provided consent or where permitted by applicable law.

California residents have the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of their personal information. See Section 10 for how to exercise this right.

5.4 With Your Consent

We may share your data with third parties for other purposes when you have given us explicit, informed consent to do so.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data is transmitted over HTTPS using TLS 1.2 or higher encryption. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plain-text passwords and cannot recover them. Prop firm API connections use read-only keys or tokens where available, and we never store your prop firm login credentials. Access to production databases is restricted to authorized personnel and protected by multi-factor authentication. Our infrastructure is hosted on SOC 2 compliant platforms (Supabase/AWS and Vercel), and we conduct regular reviews of our security practices to keep them current.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at propdesksupport@gmail.com.

7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

PropDesk uses cookies and similar technologies to operate the platform and improve your experience. We use authentication cookies — session tokens issued by Supabase — to keep you securely logged in; these are essential to the operation of the Service and cannot be disabled. We use preference storage in the form of local storage entries to remember your dashboard layout, display settings, and UI preferences between sessions. We also collect analytics data in anonymized or pseudonymized form to understand which features are used most, identify friction points, and prioritize improvements. We do not use invasive advertising trackers.

You can clear or block cookies at any time through your browser settings. Disabling authentication cookies will prevent you from staying logged in. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or retargeting pixels.

8. Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) setting that signals to websites that you do not want to be tracked. Currently, there is no universally accepted standard for how websites should respond to DNT signals. PropDesk does not alter its data collection practices in response to DNT signals at this time. If an industry standard is adopted, we will update this policy accordingly.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, email us at propdesksupport@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You have the Right of Access to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you and information about how we process it. You have the Right to Rectification to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data, and you may also update most information directly from your account settings. You have the Right to Erasure (the "Right to be Forgotten") to request deletion of your account and all associated personal data, which we will process within 30 days subject to our legal obligations to retain certain data as described in Section 12. You have the Right to Data Portability to request an export of your trading logs, account data, and journal entries in a machine-readable format such as CSV or JSON. You have the Right to Restrict Processing in certain circumstances such as while we investigate an accuracy dispute, and the Right to Object to processing based on legitimate interests including direct marketing — we will stop processing immediately upon receipt of a valid objection. Where processing is based on consent, you have the Right to Withdraw Consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing. If you believe we have violated your privacy rights, you have the Right to Lodge a Complaint with your local data protection authority — for EEA users this is your national supervisory authority, and for UK users this is the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) grants you additional rights regarding your personal information.

Categories of Personal Information Collected. The categories of personal information we collect include identifiers such as your name, email address, and IP address; commercial information including your subscription plan and billing history; internet or other electronic network activity such as usage data and session data; financial data you enter including trading account information and drawdown levels; and inferences drawn from the above to understand how you use the platform.

Your CCPA/CPRA Rights. As a California resident, you have the Right to Know — you may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you over the past 12 months. You have the Right to Delete personal information we have collected, subject to legal exceptions, and the Right to Correct any inaccurate personal information we hold. You have the Right to Opt-Out of the Sale or Sharing of your personal information — to exercise this right, email us at propdesksupport@gmail.com with "Do Not Sell My Data" in the subject line and we will process your request within 15 business days. We do not use sensitive personal information for purposes beyond those necessary to provide the Service. We will not deny you service, charge you more, or provide you a lesser level of service because you exercised any of your CCPA/CPRA rights.

To submit a verifiable consumer request, email us at propdesksupport@gmail.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line. We will verify your identity before processing requests.

11. International Data Transfers

PropDesk is operated from the United States. If you are accessing the Service from outside the United States — including from the European Union, European Economic Area, or United Kingdom — your personal data will be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States.

The United States may not have data protection laws equivalent to those in your country. When we transfer personal data from the EEA, UK, or Switzerland to the US, we rely on appropriate transfer mechanisms, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission and transfers to service providers who are themselves certified or compliant with applicable transfer frameworks.

By using the Service, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and its processing there in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

12. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide you with the Service. Account data is retained for the life of your account plus 30 days after deletion to allow for recovery requests. Trading data and journals are retained for the life of your account and deleted within 30 days of account deletion, unless you request an export first. Billing records are retained for up to 7 years as required by US federal and state tax and accounting laws. Support communications are retained for up to 3 years to assist with recurring issues or legal disputes, and server logs are retained for up to 90 days for security and debugging purposes. Anonymized analytics data that cannot identify you may be retained indefinitely to improve the platform.

After the applicable retention period, we securely delete or anonymize your personal data.

13. Account Termination

You may delete your PropDesk account at any time from your account settings or by contacting us at propdesksupport@gmail.com. Upon account deletion, your account and associated personal data will be queued for deletion within 30 days. You will lose access to all trading data, journals, and platform history, so we strongly recommend exporting your data before deleting your account. Billing records will be retained as required by law as described in Section 12. If you have an active paid subscription, cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period and no partial refunds are issued unless required by law. Anonymized, aggregated data derived from your usage may be retained to improve the platform but cannot identify you.

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate our Terms of Service. In such cases, we may retain records related to the violation as permitted by law.

14. Business Transfers

If PropDesk is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, bankruptcy, receivership, sale of company assets, or transition of service to another provider, your personal data may be transferred as part of such a transaction. In such an event, we will notify you via email or a prominent notice on our website before your personal data becomes subject to a different privacy policy. The successor entity will be required to honor this Privacy Policy or provide you with the opportunity to opt out before applying materially different terms.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 18 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at propdesksupport@gmail.com and we will take prompt steps to delete such information from our systems.
