Commonly Asked Questions

Quick answers before you reach out

How Do I Connect My Prop Firm? +
Head to the Partners page, find your firm, and click Connect. PropTracker auto-loads all your rules and account parameters — no manual setup needed.
Is My Trading Data Secure? +
Yes. We use read-only API connections where available and never store your login credentials. We have no ability to place trades on your behalf.
Can I Use PropTracker For Free? +
Yes — our Starter plan is free forever. You get one prop firm account with full rule tracking, payout tracking, and calendar. No credit card required.
My Firm Isn't Listed — Can You Add It? +
Absolutely. Send Us A Message using the form above with the firm name and we'll prioritize it. You can also ask the firm to reach out directly at propdesksupport@gmail.com.
How Do I Cancel My Subscription? +
You can cancel anytime from your account settings. There are no cancellation fees and your account drops to the free Starter plan at the end of your billing period.
Do You Offer Refunds? +
We offer a 3-day free trial on paid plans so you can try before committing. If something goes wrong on our end, reach out and we'll make it right.
What Prop Firms Are Supported? +
We currently support Apex Trader Funding, Topstep, Take Profit Trader, MyFundedFutures, FundedNext, Lucid Trading, and Tradeify. More firms are added regularly — check the Partners page for the full list.
